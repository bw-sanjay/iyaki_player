// 01 — Capabilities

Built for
every frame,
every format.

01
Universal Formats

MP4, MKV, WebM, AVI, MOV, MP3, FLAC, WAV, AAC and dozens more. Play any file without conversion or codec hunting.

02
On-Device OCR

Powered by Tesseract.js. Pause on any frame, extract and copy text directly from video in seconds.

03
Theater Dimming

Immersive mode dims the entire surrounding UI after a short delay. Pure cinematic focus, zero distractions.

04
Audio Equalizer & Vis

Studio-grade EQ with 6 presets (Flat, Bass, Treble, Vocal, Dance, Rock) and Bass/Treble adjustments, alongside a dynamic level visualizer.

05
Subtitle Engine

Custom SRT and VTT rendering. Auto-convert subtitles, adjust size, color, background, and drop-shadows.

06
Live Video Tuning

Calibrate brightness, contrast, saturation, and subtitle sync in real-time, sorted inside Tuning, Playback, and Engine settings tabs.

07
Persistent Library

IndexedDB library with Videos/Audio tabs, size, duration, container details, and real-time L/R/V audio level meters.

08
Multi-Track Audio

Switch between multiple embedded audio tracks, commentary channels, and primary streams with a single-click selection popup.

09
Network Streams

Stream media directly from URLs. Local files or network sources — Iyaki handles them all seamlessly.

10
Seek Thumbnail Preview

Hover over the progress bar to see a live video frame preview at that exact timestamp. Jump to any moment with total visual confidence.

11
Waveform Visualizer

A real-time audio waveform popup appears on progress bar hover, rendering the audio shape of any moment. Read your media before you hear it.

12
Recent Files Panel

The floating Recent panel shows your last played Videos and Audio with thumbnail previews, duration badges, and one-click resume — right inside the player.

13
Cinematic Pause Overlay

On pause, a full-screen overlay reveals the filename, format, duration, and enterprise node info — then resumes instantly with a single click.

14
Audio Mode & Album Art

Pure audio playback with embedded album artwork, track metadata, waveform preview, format details, and a dedicated full-screen audio experience.

Seek Preview

See before
you seek.

Hover anywhere on the progress bar and a live video frame thumbnail pops up instantly, showing exactly what's playing at that timestamp. Jump to any moment with total visual confidence — no guessing required.

Live frame thumbnail on progress bar hover
Precise timestamp shown beneath the preview
Zero-lag rendering at any playback position
Works across all supported video formats
Waveform Visualizer

Read your audio
before you hear it.

A real-time animated waveform popup appears on progress bar hover, rendering the audio shape of any given moment. Instantly identify music, dialogue, and silence — visually, before clicking.

Animated waveform popup on seek bar hover
Renders audio energy at the hovered timestamp
Distinguishes music, speech, and silence visually
Composited cleanly over the player UI
Recent Files

Resume instantly,
never lose your place.

The floating Recent Files panel surfaces your last-played Videos and Audio right inside the player window. Thumbnail previews, duration badges, and one-click resume — no file browser needed.

Videos and Audio tabs within the floating panel
Thumbnail previews with duration badges for each file
One-click resume at your last playback position
Clear history button for a fresh start
Pause Overlay

Pause with
cinematic presence.

The moment you pause, a full-screen cinematic overlay takes over — revealing the filename, format, duration, resume timestamp, and enterprise node. Tap play and the system resumes instantly, primed for high-speed delivery.

Full-screen overlay with file name and format details
Resume timestamp displayed with node status
Enterprise node indicator for network playback
Single-click resume with zero buffering delay
Library + Equalizer

Your library,
your sound.

The persistent media library sidebar organizes your Videos and Audio with metadata details, real-time L/R/V audio level meters, and size/duration info. The integrated EQ panel with Bass, Treble sliders and 6 presets lets you shape the sound to perfection.

Persistent IndexedDB library with Videos and Audio tabs
Real-time L/R/V audio level meters
Bass & Treble EQ with Flat, Bass, Treble, Vocal, Dance, Rock presets
Container, codec, sample rate, and size metadata display
Audio Mode

Pure audio,
full experience.

When playing audio files, Iyaki shifts into a dedicated full-screen audio experience. Embedded album art fills the canvas, track metadata and format details are prominently displayed, and a waveform preview rides the seek bar — cinema for your ears.

Full-screen album artwork display with rich metadata
Artist, album, year, bitrate, and codec at a glance
Waveform preview on seek bar hover in audio mode
Dedicated playback controls with shuffle, repeat, and skip
